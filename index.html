How to Get Started

1. Download Trezor Suite

Get the Trezor Suite for the best experience, or use the web app by clicking "Continue in Browser".

2. Install the App

Install the desktop app on your computer. Web app users can skip this step.

3. Set Up Your Trezor

Connect your device and follow the instructions to set up your Trezor hardware wallet. This process takes about 15 minutes.

4. Transfer Your Coins

Secure your crypto assets. Follow our guide to transfer your coins from an exchange to your Trezor.
